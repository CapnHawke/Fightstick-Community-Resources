\# 3D Models



Here are some 3d models which have been contributed from various people and used for assorted fightstick repairs or modifications.



\# Directory



* Lever Mods
* &nbsp;	\[LED Spacer](https://www.tinkercad.com/things/9wnfOBtzHNn-led-spacer?sharecode=4Dy7g4pXAK1mhzRtEYZvWqNjpXujeOrmbNvl45OC2wA) This model was originally made available through TinkerCad. Attribution to Brian Vieyra. The model was created to work with my \[Arcade Joystick LED Board](https://github.com/CapnHawke/Game-Controller-Parts/tree/main/Lever%20mods/Arcade%20Joystick%20RGB%20LED%20board) to facilitate installation and to prevent contact between the soldered pins on the PCB and the metal plate of the joystick. Hyperlink to the original tinkercad project and attribution have been provided, along with a copy of the 3d model hosted here and available for download directly.
